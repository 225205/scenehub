Who we are

Unlock your full potential on Scenehub, the premier platform designed to elevate aspiring actors to new heights of excellence.

Founded in 2024 by the dynamic team of Anouck de Blauwe, Evan Trommelen, Jurian Willemen, and Bas Schepers, Scenehub isn't just a network—it's a transformative community where talent meets opportunity.

Join us and immerse yourself in a world of collaboration, growth, and endless possibilities.

Why our values?

At SceneHub, our values are more than just words on paper; they are the guiding principles that shape everything we do, every decision we make, and every interaction we have with our community. Let me share with you the story of how these values come together to create a vibrant and inclusive online platform for aspiring theatre practitioners.
